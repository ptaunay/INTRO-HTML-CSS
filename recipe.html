Duck Rice



  • 1 duck
  • Rice
  • Seasoning with whatever you want
  1. Kill the duck
  2. Gather the rice
  3. Put the rice and the duck togther
  4. Next its oven time! Depends how crispy you like it
  5. Remove it from the oven and put some leafs on it to look fancy
  6. Eat it, or throw it on the thrash cause it probably tastes awfull
